Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 April 2026

AajKya ("we", "our", or "us") is a meal-planning app built for Indian households. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • Phone number — used for account authentication via OTP (processed by Firebase Authentication).
  • Physical details — age, gender, height, weight, and activity level — provided during onboarding to calculate personalised nutrition targets.
  • Meal preferences — diet type (veg/non-veg), cuisine preferences, and primary health goal — used to generate your meal plans.
  • Family information — family name and invite code — used to enable shared meal planning within a household.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Generate personalised weekly meal plans and nutrition recommendations.
  • Calculate BMR, TDEE, and daily calorie targets based on your physical profile.
  • Enable family members to share a common meal plan.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on our servers and transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Access to your account is protected by JWT-based authentication with automatic token refresh.

We do not store your OTP or Firebase credentials — authentication is handled entirely by Firebase Authentication, which is governed by Google's privacy policy.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only external service we use is Firebase Authentication (by Google) for phone number verification.

5. Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage patterns (e.g., which screens are visited, crash reports). This data is anonymised and cannot be used to identify you personally.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email address below. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

AajKya is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
